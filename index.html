🚀 Step 1: Brainstorming (The Drafting)

Before submitting, talk with your partner about these 3 key points:

1. The "What if?": Choose a change. Will you change the ending? The setting (time/place)? Or the perspective (who tells the story)?
2. Cornell Evidence: Find a specific quote or detail in your Cornell Notes that connects with your idea. Example: "In Chapter 3, the protagonist says he hates water, so my remix is about a flood."
